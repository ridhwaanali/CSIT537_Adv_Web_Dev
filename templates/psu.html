On This Page You Will Learn About The PSU

The PSU, or Power Supply Unit, is responsible for distrubiting power to all components of the computer

PSUs are usually assembled as a box and have a fan that exhausts out the heat generated

It comes with all the power cables to power on the motherboard, HDD/U.2 SSD, and GPU

PSUs are usually placed on the bottom of the computer chasis and the cables are ran from the bottom to the top
